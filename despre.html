Sons of Anarchy Motorcycle Club Redwood Original, or as it is better known, SAMCRO (often spelled Sam Crow), is the mother charter of the Sons of Anarchy Motorcycle Club. Located in Charming, California, the club was founded in 1967 by John Teller, Piney Winston, Clay Morrow, Lenny 'The Pimp' Janowitz, Keith McGee, Wally Grazer, Thomas 'Uncle Tom' Whitney, Chico Villanueva and Otto 'Lil' Killer' Moran (First 9).
